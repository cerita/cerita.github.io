About

Hi, I'm Cerita and I am a Web Developer.

I received my Bachelor in Computer Science from Saint Xavier University in 2012. The program curriculum emphasized problem-solving and computer science theory, using Java as a tool. I started my post-grad career as a Technology Support Specialist where I was able to put my problem-solving skills to use on a daily basis.

I have since developed a passion for front-end web development, and have taught myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My toolset also includes jQuery, AJAX, Twitter Bootstrap, and Sass. I am currently following the curriculum at Free Code Camp with the goal of expanding my skills to Full Stack Web Development. I also write about what I'm learning and working on at Cerita Does IT, my blog.
